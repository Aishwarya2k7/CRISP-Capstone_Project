HELP!

❝The summarizer outputs a summary from video transcripts which are large usually to save time. User enters a video id along with the number of lines in which you want the summary and the tool gives you a shortened copy for you to read. You will also get a translated summary in the output. This implementation has a German translator. But has future scope in giving the user the option to choose from.❞

Steps to Follow :-
  1. Upload Video:
    ✓   Option 1: Enter your video id
    ✓   Option 2: Upload the video. Just drag & drop, it’s super easy
  2. Number of lines: Enter the number of lines based upon how long you want the summary to be.
  3. Summarization and Translation of Video: Click ‘Submit’ to automatically generate a summary. Then click ‘Translate’ and select your desired language.

How does it work?
Using natural language processing and machine learning, we produce a summary and its translation for the video transcript given or the video uploaded.The tool has 5 phases:
  1. Audio extraction from the video if video is uploaded and then extract sentences, or direct sentence extraction from the transcript.
  2. Identification of the relevance of the words in the text
  3. Select sentences with a high number of relevant words and score them (We use Text Rank algorithm).
  4. Summarize based on the score obtained
  5. Translate into another language
